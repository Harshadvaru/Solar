    Solar Power Is Now More Affordable Than Ever!

    You can now save even more on energy costs thanks to the Federal Solar Tax Credit (ITC). The ITC allows you to deduct 26% of the cost of installing a solar energy system from your federal taxes. This applies to both commercial and residential systems, and there is no cap on its value.

    The state of Georgia has also implemented a policy called Net Metering, which allows you to sell excess solar electricity you put on the grid back to participating utilities companies.
